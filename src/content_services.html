Appointments

To book an appointment, please use our Online Booking System or call reception on (074) 93 74262.

THERE IS A €20 CHARGE FOR THOSE WHO DO NOT ATTEND A PRIVATE APPOINTMENT.

Cancellations

If you cannot attend your appointment, please let us know as soon as possible (minimum 24 hours notice) so we can offer the appointment to someone else.

Prescriptions

You can order your repeat prescription by using our Online Prescription Form.

Please allow 48 hours (2 working days) for your prescription to be processed and sent to your pharmacy.

Test Results

If your results are satisfactory you will receive a text explaining this. If we need to repeat some tests or make a follow-up appointment, you will receive a call from a doctor or nurse.

If you do not hear from us in 14 days, please phone for your results.

Sick Notes & Certification

If you need a sick note for work, you may need to attend the surgery in person to be seen by a GP. If you have been an inpatient in Hospital, we will need a letter from the hospital confirming your stay before we can provide certification.
